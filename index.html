The Greatest Movies of all time According to Arjun

My Top 3 Movies of all time

Interstellar

One of the most unique adventure sci-fi movies of all time with an absolute masterpiece of a climax

Oppenheimer

A clever piece of storytelling which results in an informative and thrilling depiction of the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer

Logan

The best comic book movie of all time in my opinion (The Dark Night is a close second). Amazing story with a grounded and mature depiction of Wolverine who is past is prime
